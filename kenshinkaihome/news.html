RECENT WIN

Kenshikai Dojo Dominates at National Karate Competition

A team of karate students from Kenshikai Dojo recently competed in the National Karate Competition and came out on top, winning first place in several categories. The competition, which was held over the weekend, saw participants from across the country gather to showcase their skills and compete against one another.

The team from Kenshikai Dojo, which is located in the city, was made up of both experienced and novice fighters, ranging in age from children to adults. Despite the diverse backgrounds and skill levels of the participants, the team worked together seamlessly and put on an impressive performance.

One of the standout performers from Kenshikai Dojo was a young student named Sarah, who took home first place in the under-18 kata category. Sarah's fluid movements and precise techniques wowed the judges and earned her high scores, putting her ahead of the competition.
